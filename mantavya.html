Mantavya (Debate)

Rules & Regulations

  • The language of the competition shall be ENGLISH/HINDI.
  • TOPIC: Is Morality an objective?
  • You have to speak either FOR THE MOTION or AGAINST THE MOTION.
  • TIME- 3 mins. For expressing your views + 1.5 mins. For rebuttal.
  • The participants are required to send aSYNOPSIS of their speech in about 250-300 words latest by 19thFebruary 2014
  • 10 best participants selected on the basis of evaluation of SYNOPSIS will be allowed to participate in the main event.

Prizes

  • First Prize: 1,500 INR + Certificate + Shield
  • Second Prize: 1,000 INR + Certificate + Shield
