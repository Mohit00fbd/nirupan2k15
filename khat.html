Likhe Jo Khat Tujhe

Rules & Regulations

Participants will be provided with 30 mins. For writing a LOVE LETTER based on any ONE topic (listed below) either in Hindi or English.

    Love letter after breakup
    Undelivered love letter

  • Size of the story should lie between 250-400 words.
  • It may include few poetic lines or verses. However as a whole it would be evaluated as LOVE LETTER.
  • Content must not hint, describe, endorse, encourage or depict directly or indirectly rape, child abuse, brutality, suicide, violence, use of drugs, sexual explicitness, hurting any specific individual’s or group’s feeling, hate speech or any such unlawful or antisocial act.
  • LOVE LETTER must be ORIGINAL and must NOT be published anywhere.
  • LOVE LETTER must NOT include email, phone number, address or any contact detail.Best entry will be asked to speak his/her letter on stage.
