NFS Most Wanted

There will be three rounds in this game – Prelims, Semifinal and Final.

Tracks

Tracks for semifinal and final will be chosen from the following

  • Sprint –clubhouse and lennox , Forest green, bay bridge and seaside , Ironhorse and coast , Stadium and chase , Diamond park , Hwy 99 and projects.
  • Circuit –Hastings , Oil refinery , Camden tunnel , Diamond.
  • Drag –Boardwalk and Bayshore, reversible and terrace , ocean and harbor.

Prelims

  • This is the qualifier round and will be based on timings. This will contain race no. 33 , 35 & 37.
  • Top sixteen players will be entertained for further rounds.

Semifinal

  • There will be four groups in this round, each containing four players and winner will be selected from each of the four groups for final.
  • There will be sprint race in the semifinal.

Final

  • There will be three types of races in final: Sprint, Circuit & Drag.
  • Points distribution in the race will be as follows
    • Player at position 1st will get 5 points
    • Player at 2nd will get 3 points
    • Player at 3rd will get 1 point
    • Player at last will get 0 point
  • Player with the maximum points at the end of three races will be the winner.

    • Rules & Regulation

    • Players must be present in the tournament area at the notifications schedule. Decision of the organizers will be final.
    • Players may bring their own accessories.
    • Use of scripts, hexcodes, trainers etc. is not allowed and anyone found using them will be immediately disqualified.
    • Porsche Carrera GT and BMW M3 cars are not allowed.
    • Collision detection: off
    • Performance matching: off
    • Junkman parts are not allowed, Cars can be tuned before racing each course.
