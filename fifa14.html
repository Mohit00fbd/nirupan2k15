Fifa '14

Format

  • Players will be drawn into two groups A & B randomly.
  • This is a Group & Knockout Format.
  • Depending on the number of players registered, the need for a quarterfinal round will be decided on the spot.
  • In Case of no quarterfinal round the Semi-finals will be played as follows
    • Semi-Final 1: Leader of group A VS Runner-up of group B
    • Semi-Final 2: Leader of group B VS Runner-up of group A
  • The choice of Home or Away, if at all is a matter of concern, will be decided by a coin toss.

Rules

  • The Player scoring most goals Wins the match and consecutively the opposite player Loses.
  • The winning Team gets 3 points.
  • In case of a Draw each team gets one point.
  • In case of tie of Points in the Group Stage the Standard rule will be followed: The Team with higher Goal Difference, Goals For, Goals Against,respectively, will be placed higher in the Group Standings.
  • Players can choose any team except Classic XI,Real MadridC.F,FC Barcelona and FCBayern Munich
  • Before attempting a Substitution the player must first inform the Organizer
  • The Substitution SHOULD be done only during stoppage of play.
  • Pausing the game when the ball is in play is STRICTLY prohibited.
