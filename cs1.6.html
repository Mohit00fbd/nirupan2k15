Counter Strike

Counter-Strike is a first-person shooter game in which players join either the terrorist team, the counter-terrorist team, or become spectators. Each team attempts to complete their mission objective and/or eliminate the opposing team. Each round starts with the two teams spawning simultaneously.

Rules & Guidelines

  • Any disconnection issues that cannot be resolved by the players in the match must be brought to the attention of an admin immediately.
  • Any use of game exploits, cheats, or any other play deemed “unfair” by an admin may result in disciplinary action up to and including disqualification from the tournament.
  • Once the match starts, players will not be able to dispute a match on the grounds of match settings.
  • No. of rounds- 10 rounds.
  • Time per round: 2 minutes.
  • Naming convention:CT/T followed by your system no.
  • Friendly Fire: On.
  • Bring your own accessories.

Violations

  • Silent or un-reachable bomb plants – The bomb must be audible and planted in a position that is accessible without a boost.
  • Punishment for any violation will be at the sole discretion of the administrators.

Qualifiers

Death Matches

  • 5 rounds as terrorists and 5 as Counter Terrorists.
  • Map will be decided on spot.

Semi-Finals & Finals

  • 5 rounds as terrorists and 5 as Counter Terrorists.
  • Starting money: $800.
  • Magnum, Bullpup, Krieg are not allowed.
  • Map will be decided by toss.
  • Map Pool:
    • de_dust2
    • de_nuke
    • de_inferno
    • de_train

    *The map pool may be changed prior to the tournament. Players will be informed of any such changes as soon as possible.

  • Side-Selection
    • By knife round / coin toss, at admin discretion.
    • At half time, teams will switch sides.
    • Half time will be a maximum of two minutes, at which point both teams must be ready.
    • In the event of overtime, teams will switch sides and play an additional 6 rounds (3 on each side) with $2,000 start money; if the score is still tied, another overtime period will be played to determine the winner.

  • Charges: 500 per team for external participants.

Prize

Teams must understand that rules listed are guidelines to ensure fair and competitive play and are subject to interpretation by the administrators based on the spirit of the game.
